Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 8th 2024

Welcome to the Sunny Buddy Privacy Policy. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information to enhance your experience with Sunny Buddy and provide personalized features. The types of information we collect include: • Location Data: We track your location to provide real-time UV index information specific to your area. Location data is only used to offer this service and is not shared with third parties. • Quiz Data: We store your answers to quizzes (e.g., melanoma risk and sunscreen quizzes) to provide personalized recommendations and track your progress.

How We Use the Information

The personal information we collect is used to: • Provide personalized sunscreen recommendations. • Tailor the UV index and notifications to your location. • Save and analyze your quiz results to enhance your experience. • Improve the app’s functionality and features. We may use anonymized data for research and analysis to improve the app, but this data will not be personally identifiable.

Sharing Your Information

Your personal information is never sold to third parties. However, we may share data with trusted service providers who assist in operating the app, as long as they agree to keep this information confidential and comply with privacy regulations. We may also disclose your information when required by law or to protect the safety, rights, or property of Sunny Buddy users or the public.

Data Storage & Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal information. Your data is stored securely on our servers, and we take measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to: • Access and update your information: You can view and edit your personal details at any time in the app. • Opt out of location tracking: You can disable location tracking through your device’s settings, though this may limit the functionality of the app.
